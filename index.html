The Xbox Store is an online marketplace operated by Microsoft for purchasing digital content such as games, downloadable content (DLC), apps, and movies. It's accessible through Xbox consoles, Windows PCs, and mobile devices, offering a wide range of titles for Xbox One, Xbox Series X|S, and backward-compatible games. Users can browse, purchase, and download content directly to their devices, often with exclusive deals and discounts for Xbox Live Gold and Xbox Game Pass subscribers.
